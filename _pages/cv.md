Research experience
======
* 2024-2026: Research Assistant Contract
  * Oceanographic Centre of Murcia (San Pedro del Pinatar) - [Spanish Institute of Oceanography](https://sscc.ieo.csic.es/)
  * Duties includes: Conducted ecotoxicological research using Callinectes sapidus, including organism maintenance, contaminant exposure experiments, dissection, hemolymph sampling, primary cell culture preparation, and fluorescence microscopy for microplastic quantification and biomarker analyses. Contributed to the development and optimization of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), analysed experimental data using statistical methods, and supervised four undergraduate internship students by providing training in laboratory techniques, experimental procedures, data analysis, and scientific report preparation.
  * Supervisor: Concepción Martínez Gómez

* January 2024 - May 2024: Student intern
  * Oceanographic Centre of Murcia (Fuengirola) - [Spanish Institute of Oceanography](https://sscc.ieo.csic.es/)
  * Duties included: Conducted biological sampling of commercial marine species, including fish, cephalopods, crustaceans, bivalves, and discarded species. Processed Octopus vulgaris samples for reproductive and histological studies through tissue fixation, paraffin embedding, microtome sectioning, and histological staining. Analysed reproductive and biological data using statistical and image analysis tools, including ZooScan and EcoTaxa, and contributed to laboratory and fieldwork, fisheries research, and scientific outreach activities.
  * Supervisors: Sámar Saber Rodríguez

* January 2023 - April 2023: Student intern
  * Oceanographic Centre of Murcia (San Pedro del Pinatar) - [Spanish Institute of Oceanography](https://sscc.ieo.csic.es/)
  * Duties Included: Participated in the design and implementation of ecotoxicological microcosm experiments with Callinectes sapidus and Mytilus galloprovincialis, including aquarium maintenance, water quality monitoring, experimental feeding, biometric measurements, dissections, and tissue sampling. Processed biological samples for biochemical and cellular analyses, including hemolymph extraction, primary hemocyte culture preparation, fluorescence microscopy for microplastic quantification, cell viability and protein concentration assays, and enzymatic activity measurements (AChE and EROD). Contributed to data analysis using statistical methods and to the development and optimization of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
  * Supervisor: Concepción Martínez Gómez

* 2022 - 2023: Student internship at the Department of Physiology
  * University of Murcia

* June 2022 - August 2022: Student intern
  * Oceanographic Centre of Murcia (Mazarrón) - [Spanish Institute of Oceanography](https://sscc.ieo.csic.es/)
  * Conducted histological analyses of Thunnus thynnus samples, including microphotographic imaging and documentation of histological specimens. Identified fish species based on morphological characteristics, cultured and maintained fish larvae under controlled laboratory conditions, and performed zooplankton identification and abundance assessments. Managed and analysed biological datasets while contributing to laboratory and research activities in marine ecology and fisheries science.
  * Supervisor: Sámar Saber Rodríguez

* 2020 - 2021: Student internship at the Department of Agricultural chemistry, geology and soil science
